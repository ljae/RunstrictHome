4. The Game — Rules and Fair Play

4.1 How Territory Works

RunStrict uses Uber's H3 hexagonal grid system (resolution 9, ~175m edge) to define geographic territory. When you run through a hex at an eligible pace (≤8:00 min/km, ≤25 km/h), it flips to your team's color. Territory is temporary — every 40-day season ends in "The Void," resetting all hexes globally.

4.2 Season Duration

Each season lasts approximately 40 days. The exact end date is announced in-app at the start of each season. No compensation is provided for territory or points lost at The Void — this reset is a core game mechanic.

4.3 Points and Leaderboards

Points are calculated based on hex flips, run distance, pace consistency, and applicable team multipliers. Leaderboard rankings are for entertainment purposes only and confer no monetary or tangible value.

4.4 Anti-Cheat

We actively enforce fair play through GPS accuracy checks (≤50m), speed caps (25 km/h), accelerometer verification, and server-side validation. Suspected cheating may result in point removal, account suspension, or permanent ban without prior notice.
