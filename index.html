RunStrict — Free GPS Running Game for iOS and Android

RunStrict is a free location-based GPS running game available on iOS (Android coming April 2026). Players physically run through hexagonal territory tiles (~175m each, powered by Uber's H3 grid) to capture them for their team. Three teams compete: FLAME (Red, elite meritocracy with 4x multipliers), WAVE (Blue, collective district bonuses), and CHAOS (Purple, join anytime but irreversible). Every 40 days, The Void resets all territory and a new season begins. RunStrict features GPS anti-cheat with accelerometer verification, battery-optimized 0.5 Hz polling, tiered leaderboards (Zone, District, Province), and a Pace Consistency Score. Unlike fitness trackers like Strava or Nike Run Club, RunStrict turns every run into competitive territorial warfare. Unlike Pokemon GO, it requires actual running with pace verification.

Every feature earns its place.

GPS Anti-Cheat

25 km/h cap. Accelerometer verification. GPS ≤50m accuracy. No spoofing. Real runs only.

🔋

Battery Optimized

0.5 Hz GPS polling. No WebSocket drains. Data syncs only on launch, resume, and run completion.

📊

Pace Consistency Score

CV-based Stability Score rewards steady runners. Green ≥80. Yellow ≥50. Red means chaos.

🏅

Tiered Leaderboards

Privacy First

No runner IDs stored in hexes. Only team color and minimal timestamp for conflict resolution.

Uber's H3 at resolution 9 — ~175m edge hexagons. Global, precise, beautiful on any map.

Got questions?

Everything you need to know before your first run.

RunStrict is a location-based GPS running game where your physical runs capture hexagonal territory tiles on a real map of your city. Choose a team — FLAME, WAVE, or CHAOS — flip hexes, and battle for dominance before the 40-day season resets in The Void.

GPS tracks your route in real-time. Every ~175m hexagonal tile you physically run through flips to your team’s color. The last runner through a hex controls it. Maintain under 8 min/km pace to activate capture mode. No spoofing — accelerometer verification blocks cheaters.

Yes — RunStrict is completely free. Available now on the App Store for iOS. Android (Google Play) is coming April 2026 — join the waitlist to get notified.

The Void is the season-end reset event every 40 days. All hexes, points, and territorial control are wiped completely clean. Only the season leaderboard legend survives. Then a brand new 40-day season begins — same map, zero territory.

FLAME if you’re a competitive elite — top 20% earn 4× multipliers through pure performance. WAVE if you love collective victory — your whole district wins together. CHAOS if you want to join anytime with no conditions — but choose carefully. It’s irreversible.

RunStrict supports iOS 15+ (iPhone) and Android 10+. GPS accuracy ≤50m is required for hex capture. The app uses battery-optimized 0.5 Hz GPS polling, so it won’t drain your battery on long runs.

Strava and Nike Run Club focus on personal fitness tracking — distance, pace, heart rate. RunStrict adds a competitive territory-capture game layer on top of your runs. Every run is a strategic move in a team-based territorial war where you flip hex tiles, earn buff multipliers, and compete on tiered leaderboards. Think of it as Strava meets Risk.

RunStrict shares the location-based concept but requires actual running with pace verification (under 8 min/km). There are no virtual creatures — you're capturing real territory tiles on a city map through physical athletic effort. Anti-cheat measures including accelerometer verification ensure only real runs count.

RunStrict works globally — the H3 hex grid covers every city on the planet. Territory capture works anywhere with GPS coverage. Currently available on iOS worldwide via the App Store, with Android launching April 2026.
