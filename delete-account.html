— Recommended: delete from the app
  1. 1 Open RunStrict and sign in to your account

  3. 3 Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account
  4. 4 Confirm deletion — your account is removed immediately
— What happens to your data
Profile & season data
Deleted immediately
Run history (local)
Stays on your device
Leaderboard entries
Removed within 24h
Auth credentials
Deleted immediately
— Can't access the app?

If you no longer have access to the app, email us and we'll process your deletion request manually within 30 days. Include the email address associated with your account.
