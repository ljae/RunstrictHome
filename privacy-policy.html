Summary: RunStrict collects GPS location data during runs to power its hex-territory game mechanics. We do not sell your personal data, do not store runner identity in hex tiles, and you may delete your account at any time.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create a RunStrict account, we collect:

  • Username (chosen by you — we recommend not using your real name)
  • Email address (for authentication and notifications)
  • Team selection (FLAME, WAVE, or CHAOS)
  • Account creation date and region

1.2 Run and Activity Data

During active runs, we collect:

  • GPS coordinates (latitude, longitude, altitude) at approximately 0.5 Hz
  • Run timestamps (start, end, pause events)
  • Pace and distance derived from GPS data
  • Accelerometer data for anti-cheat verification
  • H3 hex cells visited during the run

1.3 Device and Technical Data

We automatically collect:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • IP address (used for general region detection, not stored long-term)

1.4 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not store runner identity inside hex tile records — only team color and a minimal timestamp for conflict resolution
  • We do not collect payment information (app is free)
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or microphone

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect to:

  • Power game mechanics — flip hex tiles, calculate points, update leaderboards
  • Prevent cheating — verify GPS accuracy (≤50m), enforce the 25 km/h speed cap, and cross-check accelerometer data
  • Provide season statistics — aggregate territory data, team standings, and progress timelines
  • Improve the app — analyze crash reports, optimize battery usage, and improve GPS tracking
  • Communicate with you — send season start/end notifications and important account updates (if you opt in)

We do not use your data for advertising, behavioral profiling, or sell it to third parties.

3. Location Data

RunStrict is fundamentally a location-based game. GPS access is required during active runs.

3.1 During a Run

The app requests Always-On Location permission to accurately track your route even when the screen is locked. GPS is only actively polled at 0.5 Hz (once every 2 seconds) to conserve battery.

3.2 Background Location

Background location is only active while a run session is explicitly started by you. The app does not track your location passively between runs.

3.3 Precision and Accuracy

GPS readings with accuracy worse than 50 meters are discarded and do not contribute to hex captures. This protects against spoofing and improves game fairness.

3.4 Location Data Storage

Raw GPS coordinates are stored server-side for up to 90 days to support dispute resolution. After 90 days, only aggregated run statistics (distance, duration, hexes) are retained. H3 hex identifiers in the territory database are never linked back to individual runner identities.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

4.1 Service Providers

  • Supabase — database and authentication infrastructure (data processed in accordance with their DPA)
  • Mapbox — map rendering (tile requests do not include your identity)
  • Apple / Google — app distribution, in-app notification delivery

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or to protect the rights and safety of RunStrict and its users.

4.3 Aggregated / Anonymous Data

We may share aggregated, anonymized statistics (e.g., "X million hexes flipped globally this season") publicly. These cannot be used to identify individual users.

5. Data Retention

  • Raw GPS route data: 90 days
  • Run summaries (distance, duration, hex count): Lifetime of account
  • Hex territory records: Reset at each season's Void event (every ~40 days)
  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Server logs: 30 days rolling window

Upon account deletion, all personal data is removed within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability: Request your run history in a machine-readable format (JSON/CSV)
  • Objection: Object to processing for direct marketing (we don't do this, but the right exists)
  • Restriction: Request we limit how we use your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@runstrict.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

RunStrict is not intended for children under 13 years of age (or 16 in the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we learn that we have collected data from a child below the applicable age threshold, we will delete that data promptly.

If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us at privacy@runstrict.com.

8. Security

We protect your data using:

  • TLS 1.3 encryption in transit for all API communications
  • Encryption at rest for database storage
  • Row-level security policies in our database
  • Regular security reviews of access controls

No system is perfectly secure. In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you and relevant authorities in accordance with applicable law.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Notify you via in-app notification or email for significant changes
  • Post a banner on the RunStrict website for 30 days following the change

Continued use of RunStrict after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests related to this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@runstrict.com
  • Website: runstrict.com

We aim to respond to all privacy inquiries within 30 days.
